# IB Authorization — Canonical Record

- **F020** — Vishwashanti Gurukul World School is a formally authorized IB Continuum School. *(Source: §2.1, p.1)*
- **F021** — IB Continuum status confirms authority to deliver the full suite of IB programmes across all age groups. *(Source: §2.1, p.1)*
- **F022** — The Primary Years Programme (PYP) was originally authorized in April 2009; the certificate was reissued in November 2025. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F023** — The Middle Years Programme (MYP) was originally authorized in April 2018; the certificate was reissued in November 2025; it is authorized for Years 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F024** — The Diploma Programme (DP) was originally authorized in May 2006; the certificate was reissued in November 2025. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F025** — The Career-related Programme (CP) was authorized in October 2025. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F026** — All reissued IB certificates are signed by Olli-Pekka Heinonen, Director General (Geneva). *(Source: §2.2, p.2)*
- **F027** — The Diploma Programme (May 2006) is the earliest IB programme authorized at the school. *(Source: §6-Identity Q10, p.4)*
- **F028** — The school offers four distinct IB programmes: PYP, MYP, DP, and CP. *(Source: §6-Curriculum Q15, p.5)*
- **F029** — The school is authorized for IB for Standard I through Standard XII. *(Source: §6-Curriculum Q10, p.5)*
- **F030** — Original IB certificates feature a hologram and a watermark for authenticity verification. *(Source: §6-Curriculum Q14, p.5)*
- **F031** — The PYP covers the Primary Education Framework. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F032** — The DP covers the Pre-university Academic Framework. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F033** — The CP covers the Vocational/Career-focused Framework. *(Source: §2.2, p.1)*
- **F034** — The foundational benefits of the IB Continuum are a visionary approach to education and holistic student development. *(Source: §6-Curriculum Q12, p.5)*
