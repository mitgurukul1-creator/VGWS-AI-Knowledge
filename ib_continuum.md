# IB Continuum Status

Vishwashanti Gurukul World School is a formally authorized **IB Continuum School** — one institution authorized for all four IB programmes (PYP, MYP, DP, CP), covering Std. I through Std. XII. *(§2.1, §6, pp.1,4–5)*

- The Continuum status confirms authority to deliver the full suite of IB programmes across all age groups.
- Foundational benefits: a visionary approach to education and holistic student development.
- Earliest authorization: DP (May 2006). Newest: CP (October 2025).
- All current certificates reissued November 2025, signed by Olli-Pekka Heinonen, Director General (Geneva).
- Certificate authenticity: hologram + watermark on originals.
