## Identity & Background

**Q: What is the full official nomenclature of the institution?**
A: Vishwashanti Gurukul World School.

**Q: Which legal entity governs the school?**
A: Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Educational Research (MAEER).

**Q: Where is the physical campus located?**
A: At. Rajbaug, Loni Kalbhor, District Pune.

**Q: What is the total area of the institutional grounds?**
A: 125 acres.

**Q: Why was the 'World School' suffix added to the branding?**
A: To clarify the school's status as an IB Continuum institution and prevent confusion with MAEER's CBSE or other MIT-branded entities.

**Q: Does the institution offer residential facilities?**
A: Yes, it is an International Day and Residential School.

**Q: Who is the current Head of School?**
A: Dr. Vidhukesh Vimal, Head of School.

**Q: What is the registered address of the MAEER Trust?**
A: Survey No. 124, Kothrud Paud Road, Ex-Servicemen Colony, Pune-411 038.

**Q: What is the primary pedagogical framework?**
A: The International Baccalaureate (IB).

**Q: When was the earliest IB program authorized?**
A: The Diploma Programme (DP) was originally authorized in May 2006.

## Regulatory & Admissions

**Q: What is the status of the school's state recognition?**
A: It holds a valid No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Government of Maharashtra.

**Q: Is Marathi instruction mandatory?**
A: Yes, as a subject from Std I to X.

**Q: What is the regulatory requirement for inclusive education?**
A: 'Divyang' children must be integrated into every class.

**Q: What is the mandate regarding regional history?**
A: History of Maharashtra must be taught.

**Q: Where is the fee structure publicly disclosed?**
A: On the Government's RTE portal.

**Q: Does the school follow the RTE Act 2009?**
A: Yes, as mandated by NOC conditions 1 and 2.

**Q: How is student data tracked by the state?**
A: Through the SARAL and U-DISE databases.

**Q: Under what conditions can the NOC be revoked?**
A: If there is any deviation from state rules or instructions, without prior notice.
