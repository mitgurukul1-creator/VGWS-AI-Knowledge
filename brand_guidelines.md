# Brand & Logo — Canonical Guidance

## Official Name Usage
- **Always use:** Vishwashanti Gurukul World School (VGWS)
- **Never use as current name:** "MIT Vishwashanti Gurukul" (former name — historical references only)
- The "World School" suffix is a controlled branding measure distinguishing the school from other MAEER entities and CBSE-curriculum schools, and signals full IB Continuum status. *(§1.1, p.1)*

## Logo
- The official logo carries the Sanskrit motto: **॥ तेजस्विनावधीतमस्तु ॥** *(§6 Misc Q4, p.6)*
